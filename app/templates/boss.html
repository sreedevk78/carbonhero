Combat Rulebook

The Smog Boss feeds on CO₂. High-emission activities HEAL the boss. Low/zero-emission activities DAMAGE the boss. Choose your actions wisely, Hero.

⚔️
Actions That DAMAGE

Walking, cycling, taking the bus/train, eating vegan/vegetarian meals, and using low amounts of electricity all deal damage to the Smog Boss because they produce little or no CO₂.

💀
Actions That HEAL Boss

Driving a car, taking flights, and eating meat-heavy diets produce high CO₂, which HEALS the Smog Boss. Log these honestly — the boss grows stronger from your carbon footprint!

🔥
Streak Multiplier

Logging activities on consecutive days builds your streak combo. Your streak multiplies ALL impacts — both damage AND healing. A 5-day streak means 5× power on every log!

📊
Emission Thresholds

Ultra-low emissions (<1 kg CO₂) grant +20 bonus damage. Low (<2 kg) grants +10. High emissions (>5 kg) add +10 healing to the boss. Very high (>10 kg) adds +15 healing.
